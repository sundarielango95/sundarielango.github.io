About Me

I am a neuroscientist specializing in computational modeling and rehabilitation strategies for motor impairments, particularly in stroke patients. My work leverages machine learning, computer vision, and virtual reality to develop innovative tools and systems for healthcare and recovery.

Projects

RL-based Model for Assisting Stroke Patients

June 2024 – Present

Building a reinforcement learning (RL) model to play a virtual tennis game designed for stroke rehabilitation. The model anticipates and assists patients during recovery by co-playing the game.

Keywords: GPU, Python, Anaconda, peaceful pie module, DQN

Hierarchical Motor Learning Network

June 2021 – Dec 2023

Developed a hierarchical motor network to study skill transfer between arms under visuomotor rotation adaptation. Investigated parietal area computations for unimanual and bimanual movement, replicating experimental behavior.

Keywords: GPU, Python, Anaconda, Pytorch, Motor Learning

Publication: A lateralized motor network in order to understand adaptation to visuomotor rotation

Mapping Rehabilitation Therapy to Lesion

Jan 2019 - May 2021

Created a feedforward network to model bimanual reaching, introduced stroke effects, and analyzed retraining complexity on recovery. Mapped rehabilitation characteristics to patient profiles for optimal therapy selection.

Keywords: Keras, CNN, Blender, Retraining, Stroke

Publication: Interaction of network and rehabilitation therapy parameters in defining recovery after stroke in a Bilateral Neural Network

Developing an Automated Movement Evaluation System for Patients

May 2020 - June 2023

Used computer vision to create low-cost tools for motor performance evaluation. Tested on healthy subjects and patients, building a system to monitor improvements.

Keywords: MediaPipe, OpenCV, LSTM, Flip flop neurons

Publication: Automation of Fugl-Meyer Assessment

Developing a Virtual Reality Gaming System for Upper Extremity Rehabilitation in Stroke Patients

May 2019 - Dec 2024

Built VR games using Unity Engine for stroke rehabilitation, integrating computer vision for gesture recognition. Deployed in Indian hospitals, with data under analysis.

Keywords: Unity Engine, ZMQ module, Virtual Reality

Conference: A Comprehensive Gaming System for Rehabilitation of Upper Extremity Stroke

Journal: Feasibility and efficacy of virtual reality rehabilitation compared with conventional physiotherapy for upper extremity impairment due to ischaemic stroke: protocol for a randomised controlled trial

Modelling a Cortico-Basal Ganglia System for Rehabilitation in Hemiparetic Stroke

Aug 2017 - Dec 2018

Constructed a cortico-basal ganglia model for bimanual reaching under normal and stroke conditions. Implemented and compared rehabilitation strategies like CIMT, studying lesion size effects on recovery.

Keywords: Matlab, Actor-Critic network, policy gradient method

Publication: A Cortico-Basal Ganglia Model for choosing an optimal rehabilitation strategy in Hemiparetic Stroke
